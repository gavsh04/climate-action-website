What is Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that are constantly replenished, such as sunlight, wind, and geothermal heat.

Primary Energy Sources

Solar Power

Converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic panels.

Wind Energy

Uses wind turbines to generate clean, renewable electricity.

Geothermal Energy

Uses heat from beneath the Earth's surface for power generation.

Global Benefits

Low Emissions

Helps reduce global warming and carbon footprint.

Energy Independence

Reduces reliance on fossil fuels and imports.

Challenges

Renewable energy faces challenges such as storage limitations, high initial costs, and dependency on weather conditions.

How You Can Help

Start making a difference today:

  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Support renewable energy policies
  • Reduce electricity consumption
  • Switch to solar where possible
