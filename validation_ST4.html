Reflection on the Validation Process

Validating these pages caught a stray </p> tag after a <div> element that I would not have spotted visually. I also discovered that I had used an alt attribute on a <div> which is invalid; I have since moved this to the correct <img> element.

Managing inline SVG validation for the sitemap was particularly instructive, as it required ensuring the SVG attributes didn't conflict with the parent HTML5 structure.
