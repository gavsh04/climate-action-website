Choosing a green energy tariff or installing solar panels can eliminate your home's carbon emissions from electricity — one of the highest-impact actions available.

Eat a More Plant-Based Diet

Cutting red meat and dairy consumption even a few days per week significantly reduces agricultural methane and land-use emissions linked to your diet.

Cut Home Energy Use

Switching to LED bulbs, reducing heating by 1°C, and unplugging idle devices can reduce household energy bills and carbon output by up to 10% annually.

Planting or funding trees absorbs CO₂, restores biodiversity, and protects communities from extreme weather events linked to climate change.
